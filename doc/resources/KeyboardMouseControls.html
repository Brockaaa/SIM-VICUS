Keyboard and Mouse Controls

 

Normal Scene Navigation

 

Hold left mouse button

Orbit controller (camera rotates around point where mouse button was clicked)

Hold right mouse button

First person controller (mouse rotates camera around camera position)

Hold mouse button

Mouse translates camera

Left button click

Select/deselect surfaces

Scroll wheel

translate camera forward/backwards

 

 

W, S

translate camera forward/backwards

A, D

translate camera left/right (respective to camera view)

R, F

translate camera up/down (respective to camera view)

Q, E

rotate camera around z-axis

Hold Shift

slow movement (applies to keyboard and mouse)
