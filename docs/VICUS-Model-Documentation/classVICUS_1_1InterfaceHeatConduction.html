◆ OtherZoneType

enum VICUS::InterfaceHeatConduction::OtherZoneType

Defines what kind of zone is connected via this boundary condition.

If 'Constant' or 'Scheduled' are used when neighbouring zones are not calculated themselves but their temperature is known. For ground zones, i.e. in case of soil contact to the construction the heat exchange coeff. should be very high 1000 W/m2K to neglect the heat transfer resistance.
